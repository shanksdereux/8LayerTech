The Chairman and CEO

Meric B. Mara

Humble Beginnings

Meric was a young, passionate Linux enthusiast and technology geek when at the peak of his career in the corporate world, he mustered the courage to leave and start 8Layer Technologies to fulfill his dream of bringing IT solutions in the Philippines to the next level.

Armed with the strong desire to help his home country prosper through IT by creating innovative home-grown solutions, he first introduced autonomic solutions which helped bring cost-effective IT infrastructure to business owners and organizations.

Nation Building through 8Layer Building

With his dual role as the young Chief Technical Officer and Chief Executive Officer of the company, he worked doubly hard to set the direction of 8Layer to be progressive in continuously developing solutions that would create social impact. As an avid supporter and advocate of Open Source, he usually conducts free seminars and workshops to educate people, as he firmly believes that technology should be given not only to those privileged enough to afford it, but should be shared to the community with the goal of building the nation and consequently, uplifting lives.

Bettering His Best

Meric has extensive experience in technology, being one of the first RedHat Certified Engineers and Linus Professional Institute Certified (LPICs) in the Philippines. His career started as an engineer in notable ISP companies, and financial and banking institutions. From these experiences, he had mastered the enterprise network infrastructure and security of critical and sensitive data.

A decade henceforth from his humble beginnings, he has brought his company, 8Layer Technologies to be at par with large IT firms in delivering big data and blockchained solutions. Further, at the onset of the proliferation of cybercrime, Meric felt duty-bound to forge efficient cybersecurity solutions which he willingly shared with the community.

To augment his entrepreneurial skills, given his Computer Engineering degree, he got his Master’s Degree in Entrepreneurship in the University of the Philippines – Los Baños. Now armed with a more synergistic business mindset, he set out to establish several more companies, namely MaraLabs, Gordius, Mulat Media, Kampo de Mara, and became the Chairman of all these companies.

Because of his highly valued contributions in the IT industry and his generosity in sharing IT knowledge, coupled with his expertise in cybersecurity and designing secured IT architecture, he was appointed as member of the Multi-sectoral Advisory Board of the Communication and Advocacy Arm of the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP), and the Philippine National Police (PNP).

Meric is also highly active in the Academe, and is often invited as a speaker for numerous IT events. Currently, he is on the Computer Engineering Program Advisory Board of Adamson University and National University.

KahelOS XCD

About KahelOS XCD

It is an operating system that is designed to be the new generation router, caching and firewall box with CDN capability. This box is specifically designed to be a powerful routing platform with security in mind. KahelOS XCD is a highly configurable, full-featured solution specifically designed to provide best performance on every network.

How It Works

The KahelOS box functions as the extreme distribution network (XCD) which means that the contents are distributed with its mirrored data servers across different locations or data centers; bundled with the firewall and filtering access management and caching capabilities.

Because of its distributed network architecture and caching, access to the Internet is faster and more efficient. Contents of the frequently accessed sites are stored in the local servers, hence, saving the bandwidth that goes through the Internet.
